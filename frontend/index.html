How to Use the System

  1. Click "Start Monitoring" to begin the exam session
  2. Position yourself in front of the camera
  3. Keep your head facing forward for optimal monitoring
  4. The system will detect and alert on suspicious behavior
  5. Use the settings panel to adjust sensitivity

Pose Detection

  • Forward: Normal exam position
  • Left/Right: Looking away from screen
  • Up/Down: Looking up or down

Alert Types

  • Warning: Brief pose deviation
  • Alert: Sustained suspicious behavior
  • Critical: Multiple violations detected
